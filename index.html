The Training Scene

one scene present in almost any martial arts movie is the training scene. Whether it's the wax-on, wax-off training in the karate kid, or the brutal flexibility training in kickboxer, the training scene is a key part of showing the dedication and hard work these characters must go through in order to grow and achieve their goals.
